Types of Business
Cases We Handle

Business Trial Group attorneys combine an extensive knowledge of traditional commercial, corporate, and securities law, with the legal savvy only gained from years in the courtroom.

Our lawyers are experienced at handling a diverse array of business litigation cases, including:

Shareholder and Partnership Disputes

Lawsuits involving closely held corporations, officer/director malfeasance, derivative litigation, shareholder oppression, and partnership and operating agreements.

Investment Loses and Securities Fraud

Representation of investors and investment funds that have been damaged due to stockbroker fraud, misrepresentations, investment mismanagement, and other claims.

Real Estate Litigation

Representation of purchasers, sellers, brokers, investors, developers, landowners, tenants, condominium associations, and homeowners associations in all types of disputes.

Breach of Contract

Disputes involving business debts, leases, employment contracts, promissory notes, purchase and sale agreements, and many other commercial interests.

Intellectual Property Litigation

Claims such as patent, trademark, or copyright infringement; misappropriation of trade secrets; theft of client lists, and unfair competition.

Legal Malpractice and Professional Liability

Disputes involving professionals who failed to follow accepted standards, including claims for legal malpractice, accounting malpractice, other professional liability.

Trust & Estate Litigation

Representation of clients owed an inheritance under a trust or will with claims for breach of fiduciary duty, fraud, lack of capacity, undue influence, or other misconduct.

Construction Litigation

Litigation regarding construction defects, construction delays, breach of warranties, payment disputes, and architect and engineering malpractice.

Employment Litigation

Claims such as unpaid wages, commissions, or severance; breach of non-compete, non-solicitation, or non-disclosure agreements; and tortious interference.

Business Torts

Lawsuits concerning unfair business practices such as fraud, breach of fiduciary duty, conversion, civil theft, misrepresentation, and tortious interference.
